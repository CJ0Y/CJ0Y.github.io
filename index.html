“The goal of a designer is to listen, observe, understand, sympathize, empathize, synthesize, and glean insights that enable him or her to ‘make the invisible visible.”

Hillman Curtis

Previously, I worked in media production as a project manager. A major part of my work was understanding the vision of the writers and producers in order to create an environment where their stories can be realized. Creating that environment required not just mastery of the creative tools, but also interpreting communication styles and collaborating accordingly. I decided to make the jump to UX/UI Design because intuative design requires an element of storytelling and understanding how users communicate through technology.
